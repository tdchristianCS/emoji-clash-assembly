Welcome to Emoji Clash!

Emoji Clash is a simple, fun game by Michael Mankaruos where the user chooses one of 17 emojis to battle the computer. Simply scroll through the right emoji well to browse and select your emoji, then click fight, and see if you chose right. When you're ready to go again, click restart and have fun!

You can also switch to 2-Player mode to control the emoji on the left as well.
